A Caring, Qualified Physician

Jeffrey H. Singer M.D. is Board Certified by the American Board of Ophthalmology. Dr. Singer received his medical degree from the University of Virginia School of Medicine. He completed his surgical training at Duke University's Department of Ophthalmology in 1994. Dr. Singer is State Licensed in Louisiana and is a member of the American Medical Association, American Academy of Ophthalmology, American Society of Cataract and Refractive Surgeons, and the New Orleans Academy of Ophthalmology.

Dr. Singer is in private practice in Metairie and Gretna, Louisiana. Prior to opening his private practice Dr. Singer was Medical Director for Vision America of Louisiana. He is also a Clinical Instructor of Ophthalmology with Tulane University Medical Center. He has performed thousands of ocular surgeries including LASIK, PRK, AK, topical clear cornea cataract surgery, corneal transplantation, cataract surgery, glaucoma surgery, retina photocoagulation, and cosmetic eyelid surgery.

Dr. Singer treats in all diseases of the eye including cataracts, glaucoma, Diabetes, Macular Degeneration and laser vision correction, emphasizing a commitment to providing quality care. Dr. Singer is available 24 hours a day for emergency eye care.
